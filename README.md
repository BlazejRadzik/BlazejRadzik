### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm an undergraduate student in **Finance & Digital Technologies at UE in Katowice**, passionate about building robust, high-performance systems that solve complex financial modeling problems.

My primary interests include:

* 📈 **Quantitative Finance** (Tail Risk Estimation, Portfolio Optimization, Volatility Forecasting)
* ⚙️ **High-Performance Computing (HPC)** (C++17, OpenMP, SIMD, Pybind11)
* 🧠 **Machine Learning & Deep Learning** (LSTM, Time Series Analysis, PyTorch)
* 📊 **Data Science & Analytics** (SciPy, Pandas, SQL Caching)
* 🏆 **Competitive Programming** (CodeForces, LeetCode, IMC/IQC Competitions)
* 💻 **Open Source & Algorithm Design**

Currently focused on developing real-time pricing engines for derivative instruments and researching spectral dynamics in high-frequency financial time series.
